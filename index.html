Business Process

In general, business processes can be interpreted as a series of business activities to produce products and services. In its full meaning, Monk defines business processes as a collection of activities carried out by receiving input to produce quality output for customers.

Data Flow Diagram (DFD)

Data flow diagram (DFD) is an illustration of the flow of a system or program. Usually, DFD is used by application developers to simplify the process of developing information systems. All parties involved in the program will be visually depicted with DFD.
